Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01/24/2025

Welcome to Strike: Bats Attack. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you use our app and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

1. Data Collected by Unity Ads

We integrate Unity Ads into our app to provide personalized advertisements and improve user experiences. Unity Ads may collect the following types of data:

  • Device Information: IP address, device ID, advertising ID, and device model.
  • App Activity: Interaction with ads, clicks, views, and app usage patterns.
  • Crash Data and Diagnostics: Logs and diagnostic information to identify and resolve technical issues.
  • Location Information: Coarse location data derived from the IP address.

2. How Unity Ads Uses the Data

  • Personalized Advertisements: Serving and tailoring ads based on your preferences.
  • Ad Performance: Measuring the effectiveness and engagement of ads.
  • Fraud Prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity.
  • Service Improvements: Enhancing ad delivery and user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Partners

Unity Ads may share data with the following third parties:

  • Advertising Partners: To serve targeted advertisements and measure their effectiveness.
  • Analytics Services: To analyze app performance and improve the overall user experience.
  • Fraud Detection Services: To ensure ad quality and prevent fraud.

For more details on Unity Ads' data practices, refer to the Unity Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

  • Unity Ads retains personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in their privacy policy.

5. Opt-Out and User Rights

You have the right to opt out of personalized advertising. To do this:

  • On iOS: Enable Limit Ad Tracking in your device settings.
  • On Android: Enable Opt-out of Ads Personalization in your device settings.

Unity Ads also adheres to the Global Privacy Control (GPC) framework, allowing you to manage your data collection preferences.

6. Compliance with Google Play Data Safety

Our app adheres to Google Play's Data Safety requirements. The data collected, processed, and shared via Unity Ads is transparently disclosed in the Google Play Store listing under "Data Safety."

7. Changes to Unity Ads Usage

If we make changes to how Unity Ads or any other service handles data in our app, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you of the changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how Unity Ads handles your data, feel free to contact us at contact@xzareentertainment.com.
