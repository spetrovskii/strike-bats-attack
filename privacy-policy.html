Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01/19/2025

Welcome to Strike: Bats Attack. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you use our app and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

1. Data We Collect

We collect various types of data to provide and improve our services, ensure proper functionality, and deliver personalized experiences. Below is a detailed list of the types of data we collect:

  • App Interactions: We collect data on how you interact with our app, including interactions with in-app purchases and advertisements to understand user behavior and improve our services.
  • Device or Other IDs: We collect device identifiers to manage purchases, serve targeted advertisements, and for analytics purposes.
  • User IDs: We collect user-specific IDs to handle transactions, restore purchases, and track ad interactions.
  • Crash Logs: We collect crash logs to diagnose and fix issues that might affect the performance of in-app purchases or ads.
  • App Info and Performance: We collect information about your app's performance, including app version and session length, to optimize our services.
  • Diagnostics: We gather diagnostic data to troubleshoot issues and enhance the functionality of our in-app purchase and advertising systems.
  • Other App Performance Data: We collect additional performance metrics to ensure our app runs smoothly and to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • App Functionality: To enable features like in-app purchases, ad serving, and to authenticate transactions.
  • Analytics: To analyze how users interact with our app, monitor app health, and make performance improvements.
  • Advertising or Marketing: To display targeted ads, measure ad performance, and provide personalized advertising experiences.
  • Fraud Prevention, Security, and Compliance: To ensure the security of transactions, prevent fraud, and comply with legal requirements.
  • Personalization: To customize your experience, such as showing relevant ads or offering personalized in-app content based on your interactions.

3. Data Sharing

We share your data with third parties for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: With payment processors and app stores to facilitate in-app purchases.
  • Analytics: With analytics services to improve our app's performance and user experience.
  • Advertising or Marketing: With advertising partners to display targeted ads and measure their effectiveness.
  • Fraud Prevention, Security, and Compliance: With services that help in fraud detection, security enhancement, and regulatory compliance.
  • Personalization: With partners to provide personalized content or ads.

4. Data Retention

  • Crash Logs: This data is not processed ephemerally; it is retained beyond the immediate need to diagnose issues, improve app stability, and for analysis.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to be informed about our collection and use of your personal data.
  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to rectification if any personal data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • The right to erasure, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to object to processing, under certain conditions.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@xzareentertainment.com.
